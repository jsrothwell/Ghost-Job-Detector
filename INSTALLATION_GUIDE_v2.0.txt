### 1. Extract the Files
- Download `ghost-job-detector-v2.0.zip`
- Extract it to a permanent location on your computer
- Remember this folder location (you'll need it to stay there)

### 2. Open Chrome Extensions
- Open Google Chrome
- Type `chrome://extensions/` in the address bar and press Enter

## Troubleshooting

### Warning not showing up?

**Check these:**
- âœ“ Are you on a supported job site?
- âœ“ Is the company in your tracked list? (Check popup)
- âœ“ Try refreshing the page
- âœ“ Check browser console for errors (F12)

**Solution:** The site might use different HTML structure. Report it as an issue!

### Can't find company name?

**Likely causes:**
- Site has unusual HTML structure
- Page still loading (wait a few seconds)
- Company name in unexpected location

**Solution:** You can manually add the company via the popup.

### Extension icon not visible?

**Solution:** 
- Click puzzle piece icon in Chrome toolbar
- Find "Ghost Job Detector"
- Click pin icon

### Auto-update not working?

**Check:**
- Is auto-update toggle ON in settings?
- Check "Last Updated" in popup stats
- Manual update always works via button

### Dark mode not applying?

**Solution:**
- Close and reopen the popup
- Check that toggle is ON
- Try disabling and re-enabling

## Uninstall

To remove the extension:
1. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Find "Ghost Job Detector"
3. Click "Remove"

Your data (warnings, companies) will be deleted. Consider exporting history first if you want to keep it!
