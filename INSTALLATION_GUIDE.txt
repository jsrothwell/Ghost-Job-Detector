## Installation Steps

### 1. Extract the Files
- Download and extract `ghost-job-detector.zip` to a folder on your computer
- Remember the location of this folder (you'll need it)

### 2. Open Chrome Extensions

- Click the "Load unpacked" button that appears
- Navigate to the `ghost-job-detector` folder you extracted

## How to Use

### Viewing Warnings
When you browse a job posting from a company known to post ghost jobs, you'll see a red warning banner at the top of the page with:
- The company name
- A warning that they've been reported for ghost jobs
- Tips on how to verify if the job is real
- A link to learn more about ghost jobs

### Managing the Company List
1. Click the extension icon (ghost) in your Chrome toolbar
2. You can:
   - View statistics (companies tracked, warnings shown)
   - Add new companies by typing the name and clicking "Add Company"
   - Remove companies from the list
   - Update the database

### Tips for Best Results
- Keep the extension updated
- Add companies based on your own experiences
- Cross-reference jobs with company career pages
- Don't rely solely on the extension - use your judgment

## Troubleshooting

**Extension not showing warnings?**
- Make sure you're on a supported job site (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
- Check that the company is in your tracked list (click the extension icon)
- Try refreshing the page

**Can't find company name?**
- The extension extracts company names automatically
- If it's not working, the site layout may have changed
- You can manually add companies to your list

**Extension icon not visible?**
- Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome toolbar
- Pin the Ghost Job Detector extension for easy access
